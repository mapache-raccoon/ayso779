AYSO places players into divisions using a school-year age matrix that follows the U.S. Soccer standard. A child's division is determined by their age during the period August 1 through July 31, which keeps players grouped with peers in the same school-year range.

For example, a child who is 5 years old on August 1 will be assigned to the 6U division for the upcoming Fall season.

This system keeps teammates closer in age, supports balanced development, and helps players stay with their classmates and friends. To find the correct division for your child, please refer to the age chart above, which uses the official school-year age guidelines.

  • How the waitlist works: We use the waitlist as a pause button while we evaluate whether additional teams can be created or if openings become available due to player withdrawals.
  • Timeline: Players may remain on the waitlist for several weeks while we review team numbers and volunteer availability.
  • If your child is activated: You will receive an email notification and will have 48 hours to complete payment and secure their spot. Please check the email used during registration and expect a phone call if we need to reach you quickly.
  • Important: Placement from the waitlist is not guaranteed. Team formation in every division depends entirely on the number of volunteer head coaches available. Even if we have enough players for another team, we cannot form that team without a coach.
  • Fastest way to open a spot: A waitlisted player may be activated immediately if a parent volunteers to serve as a head coach in a division that still needs leadership. If you are willing to coach or open to learning, we encourage you to contact us.
