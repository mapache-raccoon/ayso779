AYSO 779 World Cup Watch Party

Join us Friday, June 12, 2026 for a night of soccer, community, and fun

Join AYSO Region 779 for an unforgettable World Cup Watch Party as we cheer on the world’s biggest stars and celebrate the global game together. Bring your family, teammates, and soccer friends for an evening packed with energy, community, and fun.

We’ll have contests, games, food, raffles, and a giant screen to enjoy the match in true World Cup style.

Raffles, Contests & Games

Raffles

For every goal the USA scores, we will raffle off one free registration (up to 3 goals). If USA wins, we will raffle off 2 extra registrations. (No purchase needed for this, valid for kids tickets only.)

We’ll be running a cashless raffle throughout the event featuring a variety of prizes and packages donated by local businesses or purchased by the region.

Raffle winners will be announced at halftime and again near the end of the match.

Contests

Bring your AYSO spirit or rep your favorite World Cup nation. We’ll be selecting:

  • Most Spirited Kid
  • Most Spirited Adult

More contests may be added as we get closer to the event.

Games & Activities

We’ll have a mix of family-friendly games and activities available before the match, during the match, and outside the venue.

These will be simple, fun stations designed to keep kids and families entertained while still being able to enjoy the game atmosphere.

Food & Drinks

Food, snacks, and drinks are included with your ticket.

We’ll have a variety of simple, family-friendly options available throughout the event.

Final menu details will be shared closer to the event date.
