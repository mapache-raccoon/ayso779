⚽ Welcome!

We’re thrilled to have your family join our soccer community! AYSO is all about fun, fair play, and building confidence through the game. Let’s get your player registered and ready to hit the field.

📝 How to Register

  • New to AYSO? Click REGISTER NOW at the top right of the homepage.
  • Returning families? Click LOGIN to access your account.
  1. Create your account (parent info first, then player info).
    Please carefully check all the information you enter for errors before submitting and moving on to the e-Signature portion of the registration form; these errors are difficult to fix.
  2. Upload proof of birth (birth certificate required for all players).
    • Returning players: already saved in the system.
    • New players: click the blue box next to your child’s name and upload the document.
  3. Complete all screens, including payment, even if waitlisted.
  4. Watch for confirmation emails:
    • Waitlist confirmation
    • Payment prompt (if added from waitlist)

Payment: Credit/debit card only. No cash or checks accepted.

National Player Membership

National AYSO requires every player to have an annual National Player Membership once per membership year (August 1st – July 31st). This membership must be renewed each year for players participating in any AYSO program and is separate from Chino Hills Region 779’s program fees. The national fee is collected at registration as a separate line item.

The National Player Membership includes:

  • Player ID Card
  • All AYSO member benefits
  • Eligibility to be placed on a team
  • Insurance coverage while participating in AYSO activities
  • Ability to register for eligible programs in other AYSO Regions, Areas, or Sections during the same membership year without paying the national fee again

If your player withdraws from the program, the National Player Membership fee cannot be refunded per AYSO National policy.

Refund Deadline: August 15, 2025

  • Refunds = Program Fee minus National Fee and Transaction Fee
  • No refunds after August 15
  • Uniforms must be returned before refund is issued

⏳ Waitlist Info

  • If your child’s age group is full, complete registration through the payment screen.
  • You’ll get a waitlist confirmation email.
  • If a spot opens, you’ll have 3 days to confirm and pay.

👥 Team Formation

  • Teams are built by age division and volunteer availability.
  • Sibling placement is guaranteed only within the same age group.
  • Practice schedule requests are only honored for parent-coaches.
  • Coaches receive rosters by July 31.
  • If you haven’t heard from your coach by August 15, contact your Division Coordinator.

🙋‍♂️ Become a Volunteer!

AYSO runs 100% on volunteers, which means the program can't happen without people like you. No experience? No problem! Just bring your enthusiasm and we'll show you the rest.

Why should you volunteer?

  • Make a Positive Impact - Help kids grow through teamwork, sportsmanship, and confidence.
  • Be Part of a Community - Build friendships and be part of a neighborhood team.
  • Learn New Skills - Training and support are provided for coaches, referees, event organizers, and more.
  • Flexible Commitment - Roles for every schedule, from one-day event helpers to full-season coaches.
  • Quality Time with Your Kids - Be on the field, share their growth, and create lasting memories.
  • It’s Fun! - Enjoy the game, the camaraderie, and helping players succeed.

How to Sign Up

  1. Log in or create an account
  2. Complete the Volunteer Application
  3. Choose your role (Coach, Referee, Team Parent, etc.)
  4. Complete background check (Sterling Volunteers)
  5. Take online training (AYSOU courses)
    • Safe Haven
    • Concussion Awareness
    • Sudden Cardiac Arrest
    • Role-specific training

Some roles include short, hands-on field sessions. Once cleared, you’ll be matched with a team and added to the communication system.
