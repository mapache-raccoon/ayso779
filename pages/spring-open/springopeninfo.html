National Player Membership

National AYSO requires every player to have an annual National Player Membership once per membership year (August 1st ‚Äì July 31st). This membership must be renewed each year for players participating in any AYSO program and is separate from Chino Hills Region 779‚Äôs program fees. The national fee is collected at registration as a separate line item.

The National Player Membership includes:

  • Player ID Card
  • All AYSO member benefits
  • Eligibility to be placed on a team
  • Insurance coverage while participating in AYSO activities
  • Ability to register for eligible programs in other AYSO Regions, Areas, or Sections during the same membership year without paying the national fee again

If your player withdraws from the program, the National Player Membership fee cannot be refunded per AYSO National policy.
